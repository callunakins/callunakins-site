The Philosophy

We are neurons firing in a vast, mostly dark universe. The connections we make — the sparks — are what give meaning to the void.

Desolate Sparks exists at the intersection of beauty and emptiness. These wallpapers are quiet meditations on that truth. They are not loud. They are not merely decorative. They are invitations to remember that even in the most desolate places, something is still reaching, still connecting, still illuminating.
